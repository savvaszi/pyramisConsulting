# Pyramis: Full-Spectrum Management Consulting - Project Requirements ## Brand Identity & Tone * **Name:** Pyramis * **Positioning:** Full-spectrum management consulting for SMEs and mid-market companies in Cyprus and the EU. * **Tone:** Trustworthy, strategic, quietly premium, "boutique/partner-led". * **Visual Direction:** Modern, professional, clean, minimal, with subtle pyramid-inspired geometric accents. ## Visual Language (Design System) * **Color Palette:** * Primary: Deep navy or charcoal. * Secondary: Warm sand / light beige. * Accent: Teal or copper (for CTAs and highlights). * **Typography:** Modern sans-serif for body; distinctive, premium font for headings. * **UI Elements:** Rounded cards and buttons, soft shadows, minimal gradients, plenty of whitespace. ## Sitemap & Page Requirements ### 1. Home / Landing * **Hero:** "End-to-End Consulting for Cyprus Businesses". Abstract illustration (no stock photos). * **Services Overview:** Grid for Strategy & Growth, Operations, Digital, Finance, People, Change. * **Value Prop:** "Why full-spectrum matters". * **Process:** 4-step (Discover → Diagnose → Design → Deliver). * **Social Proof:** Sectors served & Testimonials. * **Footer:** Full details, quick links, social, language switcher. ### 2. Services (Full Spectrum) * **Detail Pillars:** strategy, operations, digital, finance, people, change. * **Engagement Models:** Project-based, Retainer, Advisory Board. ### 3. Industries * **Focus:** Professional Services, Real Estate, Hospitality, Retail, Tech/Startups, Family Businesses. ### 4. About * **Content:** Mission, "Our Story", Values, Leadership profiles, Methodology. ### 5. Insights / Blog * **Features:** Category tags, filter chips, featured insight. ### 6. Resources * **Assets:** PDF checklists, whitepapers, lead-capture banner. ### 7. Contact * **Form:** Comprehensive fields including "Area of Interest". * **Office Details:** Cyprus location, map placeholder, FAQ.
